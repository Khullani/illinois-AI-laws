05What's Coming Next

29 pending bills. Two at 2nd reading. Organizations that wait for enactment to start planning will be behind on day one.

⚡
HB 3021 — Consumer AI Deception
2nd Reading · Highest momentum
▼
Would prohibit chatbots/AI agents from misleading consumers into believing they're human. Every customer-facing chatbot needs disclosure.
Prepare now
  • Audit all customer-facing chatbots — do they self-identify as AI?
  • Design disclosure UX patterns ready to deploy on enactment
⚡
HB 4980 — Meaningful Human Control
2nd Reading · Public employers
▼
Would prohibit public employers from using automated decisions without human review.
Prepare now
  • Government agencies: inventory all automated decision systems
  • Draft impact assessment templates
⚡
SB 3262 / SB 3384 — Companion AI Safety
First-of-its-kind · High political salience
▼
Would regulate emotionally adaptive AI companions — crisis intervention, persistent disclosure, parental consent.
⚡
SB 3364 — Algorithmic Civil Rights Expansion
Beyond employment → housing, credit, public accommodations
▼
Would amend Illinois Human Rights Act to cover AI in real estate, lending, credit, and public accommodations.
Prepare now
  • Financial services: audit AI lending/credit models for disparate impact
  • Real estate: review automated valuation models
